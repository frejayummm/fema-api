⬡ = Critical Access Hospital (CAH)
Per CMS / 42 CFR §485 Subpart F, a CAH must:
• Be located in a rural area in a state with a Medicare Rural Hospital Flexibility Program
• Be >35 mi from nearest hospital (or >15 mi in mountainous terrain / secondary roads)
• Maintain ≤25 inpatient beds (used for inpatient or swing-bed services)
• Maintain annual avg length of stay ≤96 hrs per patient
• Provide 24/7 emergency care services
Note: MD & DC have no CAHs — MD has not established a State Flex Program. VA has CAHs only in rural western counties.
